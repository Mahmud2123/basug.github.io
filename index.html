Welcome To Citadel Of Learning

Bauchi State University (BASU)G is a public tertiary institution located in Gadau, Bauchi State, Nigeria. Established in 2011, BASU is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering academic excellence in various fields of study. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across faculties such as Arts, Education, Science, Social and Management Sciences, and Agriculture.

BASU is committed to promoting research, innovation, and community engagement to address societal challenges and contribute to national development. The university strives to nurture critical thinking, ethical values, and leadership skills among its students, preparing them to become responsible global citizens and leaders in their respective fields. As a relatively young institution, BASU continues to expand its academic offerings, infrastructure, and collaborations with other institutions and stakeholders to enhance the quality of education and research opportunities available to its students and faculty.
